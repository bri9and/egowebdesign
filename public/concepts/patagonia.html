What We Build

Gear That Lasts

No throwaway code. No planned obsolescence. Every system is built to endure seasons, storms, and scale.

Layered Architecture

Like the best shell systems, our stack is modular. Swap any layer without disrupting the whole. Base layer stays warm; outer layer sheds complexity.

Ironclad Reliability

Tested in production the way gear is tested on granite. 99.99% uptime isn't a marketing number - it's the minimum acceptable standard.

Fair Trade Code

Transparent pricing. Open roadmaps. We pay our contributors fairly and pass cost savings to customers. No hidden extraction.

Activist Automation

AI agents that serve a purpose beyond profit. Voice systems for nonprofits. Automation that frees people to do meaningful work.

Worn Wear Warranty

We fix what we build. Lifetime support isn't an upsell - it's a responsibility. If it breaks, we repair it. If we can't, we replace it.

1% For The Planet

One percent of all revenue funds open-source infrastructure and digital commons. Not a tax write-off. A commitment.

Environmental Action

The Footprint
We Choose

Technology has an environmental cost. We measure it, reduce it, and offset what remains. Not because it's good marketing - because it's the right thing to do.

Green Hosting

All infrastructure runs on renewable energy. We choose data centers by their carbon footprint, not just their latency.

Lean Deployments

Every build is measured for size, memory, and compute cost. We treat server resources like wilderness - leave no trace.

Open Source Commons

Core tools are open-sourced. We believe shared infrastructure is stronger infrastructure. Private profit from public code isn't our model.

Local-First Philosophy

Data stays close to the user. Less transit means less energy. Edge computing isn't just faster - it's more responsible.

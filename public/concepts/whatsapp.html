Security Architecture

Privacy isn't a feature.
It's the foundation.

Every message passes through a cryptographic gauntlet before it ever leaves your device.

Your Device

Message encrypted with a unique key generated on your device. Only your intended recipient holds the matching key.

Our Servers

We relay encrypted data we cannot read. No message logs, no content access. We are infrastructure, not surveillance.

Their Device

Decrypted only on the recipient's device with their private key. The circle closes. No one else was ever in the loop.
